Homemade Diets
[caption id="attachment_6450" align="aligncenter" width="300"] Homemade diets[/caption]
  • Many pet parents love the idea of cooking for their pets!  If you are considering feeding your pet homemade meals, please consult your veterinarian to ensure that the special meals you're making are well balanced in addition to being delicious!
  • We encourage you to also use the Balance It website as a resource for recipes to ensure nutritional completeness.  Balanceit.com can even help you to formulate at-home diets for pets with special needs, like heart and kidney disease.  Balanceit.com is a favorite tool among veterinarians in private practice and one that they want you to know about too!
  • Did you know?  There are even board-certified veterinary nutritionists that are available to consult with your veterinarian!  Let the pros to formulate a special diet specifically for your pet's needs!
